## üé® Design System (Cyber-Premium)
### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Neon Magenta (`#FF4FD8`) & Electric Cyan (`#35F2FF`) for highlights and interactivity.
- **Background:** Deep Space (`#0B0F1A`) with Midnight Panel (`#121829`) for surfaces.
- **Supporting:** Success Green (`#3CFFB0`) and Soft Glow Blue (`#6BE6FF`).

### Typography
- **Primary:** `Inter` or `Space Grotesk` (Geometric & Clean).
- **Display:** `Orbitron` (Used sparingly for high-impact headings).

### Effects & Components
- **Glassmorphism:** Use backgrounds with `rgba(18, 24, 41, 0.7)`, `blur(12px)`, and subtle borders.
- **Glow Rules:** Apply standard neon glows to active states and primary CTAs.
- **Buttons:** Gradient fills for primary actions; cyan outlines for secondary.

## üé¨ Motion & Interaction

- **Timings:** Normal (200ms) for most transitions to maintain a "calm" feel.
- **Feedback:** Shimmer effects for scanning operations and premium item highlights.
