## Core Functional Architecture
- **Expert Entry (Home):** Domain diagnosis and zero-config scanning.
- **Onboarding:** Domain ownership verification and Autonomous Red-Teaming (ART).
- **SOC Dashboard:** Active Shielding HUD with real-time defensive posture indicators.
- **AI Consultant & Shield:** Predictive Active Shielding (PAS) and a human-readable Consultant Log.
- **ML Detection Lab:** Custom model training and dataset verification.

## Success Metrics
- **Mean Time to Shield (MTTS):** Reducing the time between threat detection and mitigation to milliseconds.
- **Prediction Accuracy:** Maintaining high precision in forecasting malicious intent.
- **Autonomous Efficacy:** Percentage of threats mitigated without manual intervention.
